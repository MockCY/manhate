# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Villa owners and high-end residential customers who are evaluating premium entrance doors, armored doors, and custom door systems for private homes. They arrive with a high expectation for trust, taste, privacy, and service quality.

## Product Purpose

The site presents MANHART as a premium door brand and supports product discovery, service inquiry, and consultation. Success means visitors feel the brand is reliable, refined, and suitable for high-value residential projects.

## Brand Personality

Refined, composed, private. The interface should feel like a discreet personal concierge, not a generic online support widget.

## Anti-references

Avoid cheap-looking floating widgets, loud internet-blue chatbot styling, busy SaaS customer-service patterns, excessive gradients, and anything that feels like a low-cost plug-in pasted onto a luxury brand site.

## Design Principles

1. Make the assistance feel private and intentional.
2. Use quiet hierarchy, restrained contrast, and tactile details.
3. Let the existing MANHART atmosphere lead: deep neutrals, warm metal, architectural calm.
4. Keep interactions simple and legible, with no visual noise.
5. Prefer elegant understatement over decorative complexity.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target clear contrast, readable sizing, predictable placement, and responsive behavior on mobile and desktop. Motion should be subtle and nonessential.
